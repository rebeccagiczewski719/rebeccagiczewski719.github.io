This is the calligraphy ``Zuiun"" by Shoin, a calligrapher from Shibata City, Niigata Prefecture.
"Zuiun" is a phenomenon in which the edges and interior of clouds near the sun are colored like a rainbow.
For this reason, Zuiun has long been considered auspicious as a sign of good fortune.
