Cotton Double-face Bathrobe

A practical double-sided bathrobe with an excellent water-absorbent terrycloth towel inner and smooth velour outer layer. The outer velour layer of the robe prevents dog claws from getting caught and has been finely sheared giving it a velveteen luster and texture. The robe inner uses a dense terrycloth towel loop pile that is thicker than regular towels and has a high level of water absorption and retention. The velcro chest strap makes the robe easy to wear and will sit securely on your dog.
